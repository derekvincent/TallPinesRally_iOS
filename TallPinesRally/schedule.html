November 26th 2016

Please note that the times listed are the lastest time you should arrive in order to catch the action. To get a great sport you should arrive earlier

8:00am
Parc Exposé
Check out the competition vehicles, up close and personal, while they are still shiny and ready for action!! Walk among the cars and talk to the crews before they start the rally.
Dungannon Rec. Centre / Rally HQ

Ceremonial Start of the Rally
All the competitors are introduced as they depart from the Start Ramp. A great photo opportunity!
Dungannon Rec. Centre / Rally HQ

Dungannon Rec. Centre / Rally HQ

Iron Bridge Spectator Area
12:45am
Stage A7/Iron Bridge North
Catch the second pass of the cars at this spot. The stage has changed slightly from A5 for the competitors prior to this point. This time they run in the opposite direction to A5!
Iron Bridge Spectator Area

Service #2

Dungannon Rec. Centre / Rally HQ

Stage B1/Iron Bridge South

Iron Bridge Spectator Area
3:00pm
Take a Break
Visit the charming town of Bancroft, just minutes away, and enjoy the hospitality and shopping! You might want to get a warm meal before the evening stages.
Bancroft
5:15pm
Stage B5-B6/Golton 1-2
This unique area is a twisty dirt course that winds its way through field and forest. Most of the stage is visible from the viewing area, including a jump. Arrive early, as the best spots go fast! These two stages will be run back to back this year.
Golton Spectator Area
6:20pm
Service #3

Stage C4 / Old Detlor Night

Detlor Crossing Spectator Area

Finish of the Rally
The cars start to roll into the Finish. Check out the amazing machines and crews that made it to the end!
Dungannon Rec. Centre / Rally HQ
10:30pm
Victory Podium
About a half an hour after the Finish, the scoring team usually has enough information to decide on the top three competitors. Celebrate with the National and Regional winners… watch out for the champagne spray!
